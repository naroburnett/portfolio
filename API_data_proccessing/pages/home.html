Welcome to Pokemon Team Builder!

Welcome to Pokemon Team Builder, the ultimate app for all Pokemon trainers out there! With our app, you can create your very own dream team of Pokemon by searching through the PokeAPI and selecting your favorite creatures. Our user-friendly interface allows you to easily navigate through three different pages. The first page is the homepage, which welcomes you to our app and provides a brief introduction. The second page is the "Search Pokemon" page, where you can browse through the entire Pokemon database and select your desired creatures for your team. You can search for Pokemon by name, type, or even by their abilities. Once you have selected your team, head over to the third page, the "My Team" page. Here, you can view your team's statistics, including total HP, attack, defense, and speed. You can even compare your team's stats with other teams to see how you stack up. Get ready to embark on an epic journey with your very own Pokemon team!
